The Berlin Social Workshop

ABOUT

A series of one-day workshops spurring social entrepreneurship in Berlin. Multidisciplinary teams worked using Design Thinking tools to create enterprise projects tackling social issues in the Berlin context. The Social Workshop gathered students and young professionals, coaches and experts that gave advice throughout the day, and a jury of seasoned professionals.

CONTEXT

The Social Workshop had been taking place in France and Singapur back in 2012. Our small, motivated team (still five great friends!) initiated it in Berlin in 2013. Our team grew and the workshops took place regularly until mid 2014.
